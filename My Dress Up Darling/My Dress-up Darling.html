My Dress-Up Darling

Year Aired: 2022

Genre: Comedy, Romance, Slice of Life

Overview: Wakana Gojo is a high school boy who is passionate about making hina dolls. After a run-in with the popular girl Marin Kitagawa, he finds himself helping her with her secret cosplay hobby, leading to a surprising and heartwarming friendship.
